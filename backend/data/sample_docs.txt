# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy

Our refund policy allows customers to request a full refund within 30 days of purchase for any reason. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

For sale items, refunds are issued as store credit only. Final sale items marked with "AS-IS" are not eligible for refunds.

## Shipping Information

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional $15.

International shipping is available to select countries and takes 7-14 business days. Customers are responsible for any customs fees or import duties.

## Product Warranty

All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear.

To make a warranty claim, contact our support team with your order number and photos of the defect. We will provide a prepaid return label.

## Account Management

You can update your account information, view order history, and manage subscriptions by logging into your account at any time. If you forget your password, use the "Reset Password" link on the login page.

To delete your account, contact customer support. Please note that deleting your account will remove all order history and saved preferences.
