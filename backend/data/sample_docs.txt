# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy

Our refund policy allows customers to request a full refund within 30 days of purchase for any reason. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

For sale items, refunds are issued as store credit only. Final sale items marked with "AS-IS" are not eligible for refunds. Digital products and downloadable content are non-refundable once accessed.

To request a refund, log into your account, go to Order History, and click "Request Refund" next to the eligible order. You'll receive a return shipping label via email within 24 hours.

## Return Process

Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery. Items should be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with all tags attached. Once we receive your return, inspection takes 2-3 business days. Approved refunds are processed immediately to your original payment method.

If returning a defective item, we cover return shipping costs. For all other returns, a $7.99 return shipping fee will be deducted from your refund. You can also use our prepaid return label for $7.99.

Gift returns are refunded as store credit to the recipient. The original purchaser will not be notified of the return.

## Exchange Policy

We offer free exchanges for different sizes or colors within 45 days of purchase. To exchange an item, request an exchange through your account, and we'll send you the new item immediately. Return the original item using the provided label within 14 days of receiving the exchange.

Exchanges are subject to availability. If the desired item is out of stock, we'll issue store credit for the full amount.

## Shipping Information

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional $15. Overnight shipping is available for $25 in select metro areas.

International shipping is available to over 100 countries and takes 7-14 business days. International customers are responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes. We use DHL Express for international shipments.

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Track your package in real-time through your account dashboard.

## Order Tracking

Track your order anytime by logging into your account and viewing Order History. You'll see real-time updates including: Order Confirmed, Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, and Delivered.

If your tracking shows no movement for 3+ days, contact support immediately. We'll investigate with the carrier and provide updates within 24 hours.

For orders marked as "Delivered" but not received, check with neighbors, building management, or household members. If still missing after 48 hours, file a claim with us and we'll open a carrier investigation.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost in transit, contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We'll file a claim with the carrier and either reship your order or issue a full refund.

For damaged items, take photos of the packaging and product damage. Contact support within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund without requiring a return of the damaged item.

All shipments over $100 are automatically insured. For high-value orders, we require signature confirmation to prevent theft.

## Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement if they haven't shipped. To cancel, go to Order History and click "Cancel Order". If the cancellation button is unavailable, the order has already been processed and must be returned after delivery.

Expedited orders (Express, Overnight) cannot be cancelled as they're processed immediately. Custom or personalized orders cannot be cancelled once production begins.

Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.

## Product Warranty

All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, normal wear and tear, or unauthorized repairs.

To make a warranty claim, contact our support team with your order number, photos of the defect, and a description of the issue. We'll provide a prepaid return label within 24 hours. Warranty claims are processed within 5-7 business days.

Extended warranties (2-year and 3-year plans) are available at checkout for an additional fee. Extended warranties cover accidental damage and provide expedited replacement service.

## Product Troubleshooting

Before returning an electronic item, try these troubleshooting steps:
- Ensure the device is fully charged
- Check all cable connections
- Restart the device by holding the power button for 10 seconds
- Update to the latest firmware through the device settings
- Reset to factory settings (backup your data first)

For specific product issues, visit our Support Center for detailed troubleshooting guides and video tutorials. Our technical support team is available via live chat Monday-Friday 9 AM - 8 PM EST.

## Account Management

You can update your account information, view order history, manage subscriptions, and save payment methods by logging into your account at any time. Your account dashboard shows all past purchases, tracking information, and downloadable invoices.

## Password Security

We recommend using a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Never share your password or use the same password across multiple websites.

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, change your password immediately and contact support. We'll review recent account activity and secure your account.

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your password and never store passwords in plain text. Our team will never ask for your password via email or phone.

## Payment Methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. We also offer Buy Now Pay Later options through Klarna and Afterpay for orders over $50.

All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We don't store your full credit card number - only the last 4 digits for order reference.

International customers can pay in their local currency. Currency conversion rates are determined by your bank or credit card provider.

## Payment Issues

If your payment is declined, check that:
- Card information is entered correctly
- Billing address matches your card statement
- Card has sufficient funds or available credit
- Card hasn't expired
- Your bank hasn't flagged the transaction as suspicious

Contact your bank if the issue persists. Some banks block international or online transactions by default and require manual authorization.

For security, we may require additional verification for large orders or orders shipping to addresses different from the billing address.
