# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy

Our refund policy allows customers to request a full refund within 30 days of purchase for any reason. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

For sale items, refunds are issued as store credit only. Final sale items marked with "AS-IS" are not eligible for refunds. Digital products and downloadable content are non-refundable once accessed. Opened cosmetics, personal care items, and undergarments are final sale for hygiene reasons and cannot be returned.

To request a refund, log into your account, go to Order History, and click "Request Refund" next to the eligible order. You'll receive a return shipping label via email within 24 hours.

Refund processing time depends on your payment method. Credit and debit card refunds typically post within 5-7 business days, though your bank may take an additional billing cycle to reflect it on your statement. PayPal refunds usually appear within 3-5 business days. Buy Now Pay Later refunds (Klarna, Afterpay) are sent to the provider, who applies them to your installment plan or issues a separate refund depending on how many installments you've already paid.

If you paid with a mix of store credit and a card, the store credit portion is refunded first, then any remaining amount goes back to your card.

## Return Process

Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery. Items should be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with all tags attached. Once we receive your return, inspection takes 2-3 business days. Approved refunds are processed immediately to your original payment method.

If returning a defective item, we cover return shipping costs. For all other returns, a $7.99 return shipping fee will be deducted from your refund. You can also use our prepaid return label for $7.99. Large or bulky items (furniture, appliances over 50 lbs) require a $35 return shipping fee due to freight carrier costs, and a pickup must be scheduled rather than dropped off.

Gift returns are refunded as store credit to the recipient. The original purchaser will not be notified of the return.

If a return fails inspection (missing tags, signs of wear, missing accessories), we'll notify you by email with photos and give you the option to have the item shipped back to you at your expense, or to accept a partial refund reflecting the item's condition.

## Exchange Policy

We offer free exchanges for different sizes or colors within 45 days of purchase. To exchange an item, request an exchange through your account, and we'll send you the new item immediately. Return the original item using the provided label within 14 days of receiving the exchange. If the original item isn't returned within 14 days, we'll charge your original payment method for its full price.

Exchanges are subject to availability. If the desired item is out of stock, we'll issue store credit for the full amount. If the replacement item costs more than the original, you'll be charged the difference; if it costs less, we'll refund the difference to your original payment method.

## Shipping Information

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional $15. Overnight shipping is available for $25 in select metro areas. Orders placed after 1 PM EST are processed the next business day; orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the following business day.

During peak holiday periods (Black Friday through December 20), standard shipping times may extend to 7-10 business days due to carrier volume. We publish annual holiday shipping cutoff dates on our website — orders placed after the cutoff for a given shipping speed are not guaranteed to arrive before December 25.

International shipping is available to over 100 countries and takes 7-14 business days. International customers are responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes. We use DHL Express for international shipments.

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Track your package in real-time through your account dashboard. Note that tracking numbers can take up to 24 hours to become active in the carrier's system after we generate the shipping label.

## Order Tracking

Track your order anytime by logging into your account and viewing Order History. You'll see real-time updates including: Order Confirmed, Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, and Delivered.

If your tracking shows no movement for 3+ days, contact support immediately. We'll investigate with the carrier and provide updates within 24 hours. Note that "no movement" during a scheduled carrier facility transfer (visible as "Departed Facility" with no new scan for 24-48 hours) is usually normal and not cause for immediate concern.

For orders marked as "Delivered" but not received, check with neighbors, building management, or household members. If still missing after 48 hours, file a claim with us and we'll open a carrier investigation. Apartment and multi-unit building deliveries account for the majority of "delivered but not received" cases — carriers sometimes leave packages at the building's main entrance or mailroom rather than your specific unit.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost in transit, contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We'll file a claim with the carrier and either reship your order or issue a full refund.

For damaged items, take photos of the packaging and product damage. Contact support within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund without requiring a return of the damaged item.

All shipments over $100 are automatically insured. For high-value orders, we require signature confirmation to prevent theft. If a package requiring signature confirmation is marked delivered without one (carrier error), we treat it the same as a lost package and file a claim on your behalf — you are not responsible for proving carrier negligence.

## Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement if they haven't shipped. To cancel, go to Order History and click "Cancel Order". If the cancellation button is unavailable, the order has already been processed and must be returned after delivery.

Expedited orders (Express, Overnight) cannot be cancelled as they're processed immediately. Custom or personalized orders cannot be cancelled once production begins, which is typically within 1 hour of order placement.

Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method. If your card has already been charged and the order is cancelled before shipping, you'll see a separate refund transaction rather than the original charge being voided — this is normal and depends on your bank's processing.

## Product Warranty

All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, normal wear and tear, unauthorized repairs, or water damage on non-waterproof devices.

To make a warranty claim, contact our support team with your order number, photos of the defect, and a description of the issue. We'll provide a prepaid return label within 24 hours. Warranty claims are processed within 5-7 business days. If the manufacturer determines the issue is not covered (e.g., accidental damage), you'll be offered a paid repair quote instead of a free replacement.

Extended warranties (2-year and 3-year plans) are available at checkout for an additional fee. Extended warranties cover accidental damage and provide expedited replacement service, including a one-time no-questions-asked accidental damage replacement per coverage year.

## Product Troubleshooting

Before returning an electronic item, try these troubleshooting steps:
- Ensure the device is fully charged
- Check all cable connections
- Restart the device by holding the power button for 10 seconds
- Update to the latest firmware through the device settings
- Reset to factory settings (backup your data first)

For specific product issues, visit our Support Center for detailed troubleshooting guides and video tutorials. Our technical support team is available via live chat Monday-Friday 9 AM - 8 PM EST. If troubleshooting doesn't resolve the issue and the product is within its return or warranty window, our support team can convert a troubleshooting ticket directly into a return or warranty claim without you needing to submit a new request.

## Account Management

You can update your account information, view order history, manage subscriptions, and save payment methods by logging into your account at any time. Your account dashboard shows all past purchases, tracking information, and downloadable invoices.

If you forget your password, use the "Reset Password" link on the login page. You'll receive a password reset email within 5 minutes. The reset link expires after 1 hour for security. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder first; if it's still missing after 15 minutes, contact support to verify the email address on file is correct.

To delete your account, contact customer support. Please note that deleting your account will permanently remove all order history, saved addresses, payment methods, and preferences. Active orders will still be fulfilled, but you won't be able to track them after account deletion. Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days, and we retain minimal transaction records for tax and legal compliance even after deletion, as required by law.

## Password Security

We recommend using a strong password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Never share your password or use the same password across multiple websites.

If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, change your password immediately and contact support. We'll review recent account activity and secure your account. If unauthorized orders were placed, we'll cancel any that haven't shipped and treat shipped ones as fraud claims, which do not count against your return window.

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your password and never store passwords in plain text. Our team will never ask for your password via email or phone.

## Two-Factor Authentication

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in your account settings for an extra layer of security. We support SMS codes and authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy). Once enabled, you'll be asked for a verification code any time you log in from a new device or browser.

If you lose access to your 2FA method (lost phone, uninstalled authenticator app), contact support with your order history details to verify your identity. Account recovery in this case can take 2-3 business days due to the additional identity verification required.

## Payment Methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. We also offer Buy Now Pay Later options through Klarna and Afterpay for orders over $50.

All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We don't store your full credit card number - only the last 4 digits for order reference.

International customers can pay in their local currency. Currency conversion rates are determined by your bank or credit card provider, and a small foreign transaction fee may apply depending on your card issuer — this fee goes to your bank, not to us.

## Payment Issues

If your payment is declined, check that:
- Card information is entered correctly
- Billing address matches your card statement
- Card has sufficient funds or available credit
- Card hasn't expired
- Your bank hasn't flagged the transaction as suspicious

Contact your bank if the issue persists. Some banks block international or online transactions by default and require manual authorization.

For security, we may require additional verification for large orders or orders shipping to addresses different from the billing address. Orders over $500 shipping to a new address for the first time are automatically held for 24 hours for manual fraud review.

## Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If you believe you were charged in error, please contact our support team first — most billing issues can be resolved directly and much faster than through a bank dispute. If you file a chargeback with your bank before contacting us, your account may be temporarily suspended from placing new orders while the dispute is investigated, which can take 60-90 days depending on your card issuer.

Chargebacks filed for orders that were delivered as described, where the customer simply changed their mind, are contested with delivery and tracking evidence. Repeated chargeback abuse may result in account closure and a requirement to pay via bank transfer for future orders.

## Price Matching and Price Adjustments

If an item you purchased drops in price within 14 days of your order (excluding flash sales, clearance, and Black Friday/Cyber Monday pricing), contact support with your order number for a one-time price adjustment issued as store credit for the difference.

We do not price match against other retailers. We do match our own website's price across regions if you were charged a higher price due to a display error.
